Serial Numbers

 Top  Previous  Next

Serial numbers are used to uniquely identify components of HsM devices. A device can be uniquely identified by the serial number of one of its components together with the device information stored in the HsM Backend.

Serial numbers are not strictly numerical, they also contain letters and symbols. Serial numbers are not meaningless identifiers but encode  information. Serial numbers are usually printed on components of HsM devices and are usually also part of the QR Code printed on those components.

Throughout the years different versions of serial numbers have been used to identify components of HsM devices.
