The queue subdirectory is used as a queue for devices that need to be registered with the Digital Twin Service. The queue directory is filled by querying the Provider Database, and then the elements on the queue are registered one by one.

Devices that can for some reason not be registered with the Digital Twin Service will stay on the queue.
